About .DJPL

.DJPL (Digambar Jain Premier League) is a community-centric initiative to bring all age groups together through different sports while living our Jain values of respect, truth and non-violence.

Format

Multi-Sport, Multi-Category

  • Different formats for each sport (short matches, quick games).
  • Categories based on age / gender as decided by organizers.
  • League & knockout style, depending on number of entries.
  • Schedule shared sport-wise in advance.

Values & Guidelines

.DJPL is more than a tournament ‚Äì it is a reflection of our Jain values on and off the field. Please read these principles carefully.

Core Principles

  • Mutual Decision Making: Major decisions are discussed and concluded together, not individually.
  • Transparency: Clear communication on rules, fixtures, scoring, and any changes.
  • Openness: All participants should feel comfortable sharing feedback, suggestions and concerns.
  • Jain Values: Respect (maan), truthfulness (satya), non-violence (ahimsa) and humility (vinay) in words and actions.
  • Respect for Officials: Umpire / referee decisions are final; no arguments or abuse.
  • Time Discipline: Teams and players must report before match time; walkover rules may apply.
  • Zero Tolerance: No abusive language, personal attacks or behaviour against Jain ethics.
